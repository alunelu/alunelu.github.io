Alunelu Loto privacy policy

Alunelu Loto app doesn't collect any information. It is simply a tool for generating lottery numbers and downloading extracted numbers.

Alunelu Loto app shares diagnostics data to Firebase for analysing the app's usage and for improving it. The data is not personal and cannot be used to identify the users.

Alunelu Loto app displays ads via Google AdMob. Please see their Privacy Policy for more details.

Alunelu Loto app also uses Google Analytics which uses cookies, please see their Privacy Policy for more details.
