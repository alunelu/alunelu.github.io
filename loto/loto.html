Alunelu Loto

This app helps you play several world lotteries. With it you can generate random numbers (simulate a draw, play slots), keep record of your played numbers, scan lottery tickets, download extracted numbers, see your winning numbers, backup/restore numbers to/from Dropbox.

There are some games predefined but you can still use the app if your lottery is similar to one of those games except that you won't be able to download the extracted numbers.

For example, if your lottery game has 6 numbers between 1 and 49 then you can use Romania 6/49 game. You can see the list of available games by pressing the button 'Games'
